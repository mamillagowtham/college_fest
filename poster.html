Department of Information Technology (IT)

POSTER PRESENTATION

Event Rules

  • Topic must be a technical innovative idea or innovation in technical research, education, and extension.
  • The dimensions of your poster should not exceed A2 size paper (approx 25×18 in) in length.
  • The heading should have letters at least 35mm high listing the title, author’s name, institution, and location.
  • Text and illustrations should be at least 15mm high so they are readable from 1m distance.
  • Poster content should be 70% graphic (pictorial) and 30% text to describe the topic.
  • Illustrations (graphs, pictures, figures) should be large enough for easy observation.
  • The maximum time allowed for the presentation is 10 minutes.
  • Jury and audience may ask questions during or after the presentation.

Certification & Awards

  • Participation certificates will be given to all presented authors.
  • Winners will receive merit certificates and exciting prizes!

Venue & Time

IT Lab (4th Floor) @ 10:00 AM

Important Notes

  • The jury's decision regarding the winner and prizes will be final and binding for all participants.
  • The poster must be mounted on solid material like thermocol or a plastic frame.
