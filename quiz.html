Department of Information Technology (IT)

TECHNICAL QUIZ

General Rules

  • Participants can complete as a team; every team may contain a max of two members.
  • Same team members should compete for all rounds.
  • There will be three rounds in the competition; every round is a knockout round.
  • Questions in all rounds are from Computer Science subjects.

Quiz Rules

  • In the first round, participants face a direct MCQ test (paper-based).
  • The second round may include questions in the form of pictures, audios, and videos with negative marking.
  • The final round is a rapid-fire round with 10 questions in 120 seconds. The team with the highest correct answers wins!

Certification & Awards

  • Participation certificates will be given to all participants.
  • Winners will receive merit certificates and exciting prizes!

Venue & Time

IT Lab (4th Floor) @ 11:00 AM
